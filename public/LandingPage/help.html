Help Center

Find answers to common questions or reach out to our team directly.

How do I register for a tournament?
Creating an account is simple. Click "Get Started" in the navigation bar to create your profile, then link your game IDs. Browse active tournaments in the Rankings section and click "Join Arena".
Are my game statistics updated in real-time?
Yes, we pull public data continuously. Most stats are updated within 5 minutes of a match ending. However, heavily populated tournaments might experience a short delay.
How do the leaderboards work?
Leaderboards track verified match data to rank players based on KD ratio, total eliminations, win rate, and survival times. The specific weighting depends on the tournament ruleset.
Is my account data safe?
Absolutely. We never ask for your game passwords or sensitive data. We only use publicly accessible game IDs to fetch and verify your public statistics. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Still have questions?

If you need technical support, want to report a bug, or have any other inquiries, our support team is ready to help.
